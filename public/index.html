Panic In The Dark:

Panic In The Dark is a game of cat and mouse between Humans and Zombies. You are placed in a post apocalyptic hospital where the power surges every 10 seconds. Humans can only see when the lights are on, Zombies can only see in the dark. When your team has vision of the map you are the 'Hunting Team' and can kill enemy players by running into them. When your team is in the dark you must run from enemy players, or be killed. If you are killed you have to wait until the next power surge to come back into the match. You accumilate points for time survived during darkness and for enemies you've killed while your team is hunting. You do not accumliate points while you are dead.

Will you survive the human race?
